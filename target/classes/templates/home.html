What is ASIA Neurological Testing...?

Patients in the ongoing Neuro-Spinal ScaffoldTM study are required to have a complete spinal cord injury. These patients have no motor or sensory function below the level of their spinal cord injury including the sacral (lower) segments at S4-5. Patients with incomplete spinal cord injury have partial sensory and/or motor function below the level of injury.
